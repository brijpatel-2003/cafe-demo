Our Story

Where Every Sip is
A New Destination

World Travel Cafe is Kadi's most-loved travel-themed café, nestled in the heart of Hanumant Plaza. We believe a great cup of coffee or a delicious snack can take you anywhere in the world.

Inspired by the spirit of travel and the warmth of Gujarati hospitality, we've created a space that feels like a journey — walls adorned with world maps, globes, and travel memorabilia that spark the wanderer in you.

From a refreshing cold coffee to hearty snacks and everything in between, our menu is crafted to delight every palate. Whether you're catching up with friends, working remotely, or on a date night — World Travel Cafe is your destination.

Travel-Inspired Ambience

Unique décor featuring world maps, vintage globes, and travel photographs from across the globe.

100% Vegetarian

Proudly serving delicious vegetarian food made with fresh, quality ingredients — perfect for every taste.

Kadi's Top-Rated Café
